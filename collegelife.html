written by me (H.J)


tin saal guzar gae KHALSA ch
jane kinea naal hath milae mein


kuch yaar reh gae merian yadaan ch
kae veeran di jagah bithae mein


ikathe hoke lae lecture kae
bahar khalo ke zada bitae mein


kade peya rolla attendence da
te fine bhar ke saal langae mein


hamesha karae assignment late deposit
bahane te laare kine lae mein


ikathe ho lae geeri har block di
jaane kine raah paera haith haundae mein


kade vehle ho jana canteen nu
jithe aunty jo aunty kehan te tokdi c


kehndi c kao didi mainu
pata ni apne aap nu ki sochdi c


vehle baithe raena canteen ch
truth n dare di wari la laini


jande jande v kise ne
didi nu aunty di awaz laga jani


kuch teacher lag gae nawe mainu
kae purane aje v padhaunde ne


jina to kade padhea c mein
oh dujea da lect launde dis jande ne


sir jhuka ke lang jana una di class ageo
andar jake mud lecture laun de khyal aunde ne


par dekh class begani
eh ass v dil ch reh jandi


kyun kite ene lecture bunk mein
eh soch mathe te shikan fe pae jandi


last bench te jake beh jana
akhan band ja bahar e dhyaan hona


kade teacher ne v toak dena
kade dekh k vi anjaan huna


ageo kehna maito pen haini
kade ape e guma dena


kade launa bahana copy muk gae ae
fir mobile de msgan wal e dheyan huna


karni miss call class ch bakian nu
je kise ne chuk lae te ageao pareshan huna


lecture muke te janna jaroor paani peen
pani ta kade peeta ni rounak wal e dheyan huna


aj fir kalea college janda haan
sub chehre oapre lagde ne


nai gal rae pehla wali college ch
sub raaste ruseo disde ne


jis tahli thale khad de c o
aj haal sarea da puchdi ae


nai jawab maito koi dita janda
bus keh dina khush e sare lagde ne


ik naam nimana harshal ae
eh gal na dilon bhula dena


kade takkar javan kise moad ute
bus hass ke gal nal la laena


nai shikwa mainu kise de naal
bus raaste vakhre ne ho gae


kade ik duje di gul ch hami bharde c
aj milea nu ne arse ho gae


kite dis jae purana dost koi
eh tang dil vich lagi raendi ae


kalea hun time ni langda yaaro
sarea di yaad dilan vich raendi ae ..........
