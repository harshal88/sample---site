written by me (H.J)

jihde taare haani bun jande
ohnu jug di na parwah rehndi ..........


sari raat akhan jagan, hove didar mahi da
dil vich hoor na koi chaa rehndi ,
sir te rakh ghume tookra ishque da
sajjan de ghar wal e dil di raah rehndi ,


jihde taare haani bun jande
ohnu jug di na parwah rehndi ..........


saari raat satave yaad chandri
khyalan ch pehli mulakat wali e tha rehndi ,
chahe jhalne paen lakh dukh
hanjuan di naena ch na koi tha rehndi ,


jihde taare haani bun jande
ohnu jug di na parwah rehndi ..........


lakhan de kafile ch vi ghume ikalla
te mehfillan ch lakoan nu futt
chehre te jhooti muskan rehndi ,
har waqt kare warke kalle
karan nu jazbat beyan
kol alfazan di dukkan rehndi ,


jihde taare haani bun jande
ohnu jug di na parwah rehndi ..........


sikhar dupehre disse chann di chanani
wagdi looh vi sheet hawa lagdi ,
haard de mahine khape thul vich
magdi raet te paeran di zubaan rehndi ,


jihde taare haani bun jande
ohnu jug di na parwah rehndi ..........


lagi rehndi sajan de aun di udeek
lekkan nal bhari kori kand
din mahine sallan di gawah rehndi ,
samma guzre bundeya yaadan di fulkari
chite fullan piche vichore di agg wajah rehndi ,


jihde taare haani bun jande
ohnu jug di na parwah rehndi ..........


suk jande hanju , jholi haukiyan di bhari,
sath daen te bahran nu hungara
akhiyan di lalli guwah rehndi ,
yaddan de samunder ch khawe roj gotte
bahar nikle na kade , dubb jaan deo taang rehndi


jihde taare haani bun jande
ohnu jug di na parwah rehndi ..........
