written by H.J



ik din tureya java main viraane ch
aun dil ch kae sawal mere

ki khateya ake jug te main
bahute kaende kuch ni pulle tere

fir aya hawa da bulla ik
khalogi zindagi ake sammne mere

kaendi tureya janna ene wariya to
bol manjil keri kia dil ch tere

main keya aya hun yaad jado muk chali jindari
udo kithe c jado akhan ch sun athru mere

kaendi hisab hai mere kol har ik hanju da
kari yakeen ik wini wageya najayaz akhan cho tere

main keya kadi ditti mainu suzza, kade socheya na main bura
kise lae nikle na marde bol kade muh cho mere

kaendi mungda ae jawab , doshi sumjada ae mainu
kari khayal ik main e ta c,jine jhalle nal sare dukh eh tere

main keya mainu shikwa ne kae, meri fitrat te ilzam ne bade
sada parkhiyan harik ne kamiya meriyan ,kise vekhe na change gunn v mere

kaendi chaunda hai ki , khol dil wale warke
keri palli baitha gum , pata te lage kere dukh ne tere

main keya aj a gayi ae mere dukhde sunan
sari umaar na lageya koi lagge mere

kaendi karam c tere jo to kutti jindagi ikkaleya
hun aunge lokki bade magar tere

main keya na kar mayak nimani jind ae meri
pata mainu auga hajoom shamil hoan kaffille ch mere

kaendi kudi tukki kothe char ke guwand banere
milju jawab nale lag ju pata ,eh hoa ni jug te ikkalle nal tere
