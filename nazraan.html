written by me (H.J)

kade dekh neevi tu pa lae
kade sang ke nazar chura lae
na kite kade izhaar tu
bus dil ch gal chupa lae

teri kambdi hoe awaz ne
kae varke dil de khol dite
door reh ke v maito tu
meeri rooh nal sanj ik pa lae

teri har takni da hisab mere kol
kine pal mere nal nazaran milea c
kae bhaed ena ne khol ditte
kade parde ch gal ena luka lae

har kone talashea ena nazaran ne
har saah ch kita mehsoos tainu
tainu hasda vekhan lae is butte ne
chhaaan vi apni gawa lae

kade kadam tere ne dol gae
kuch meri vi majbori c
wag gea suma wang pani de
t wakh rehan di adat v pa lae

yaar mere samjaunde mainu
nai kandea nal yaari lae di
par hun gal ni mere was di rae
2hade yaar ne ta gumma nal yaari la lae
