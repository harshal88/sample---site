written by me (H.J)


mithi boor nal khushboo , jado mitti d awe ,yadaan teriyan nu , eh hulara de jave ,tapdi zameen nu , te dey jandi hai sukoon , mere dil de virane nu , hor banjar eh kar jave ,


utto wagdi thandi hawwa , jund de pateya nu hilawe , mithe haseya de sur , sare goonjan zehan vich mere , hathan ch paya lall choora , dil mera toad jave ,


vajdi vanjali sunke panchiyan di , soche sahiba de sirahne , ik jhok lag jave , buki banke jand de kinare , so gea ho befikra jatt ,aunda samay vekh ke , sahiba di jaan ghabrawe ,


akh lagi jado fe , supna sahiba da e awe , vekh apni maut di kahani , dil passej jea jave , kare ardas gura age , eh din hale na dikhawe ,


gareya di awaz sun , tuti jado neend, laun nu dhakewa , chit saun nu kari jave , par vekh khalote , muhre saheba de veer , akhan te mirje nu , aitbar e na awe ,


kalle badal jado , chare paso ghera pawe , mirje nu ghaer lea , inj halat banjave , badal guj ke jado , zor apna dikhave , ikale mirje te panje bhra , ral jive roab pave ,


ik pal lae hatey , jado ghutta eh kalli , takhat hazara nu janda , painda akhan sawe ave , kar jigra wada , tur peya c gharo, bhabhiya de taane sunan , hun kera pind moade pave ,


akhan gusse nal lall , dil ch aun kae sawal , peeke dukhan da peyala , mirje nu sahiba d fikar satawe , fir panja paniya da yodha , pawe tarkash nu hath , karan dakre ehna de , dil ch iko khayal awe ,


asmanu jado bijli di , taar ik awe , teri mukh di lishkor , nu sajda kari jave , kade jaape mirje nu , kamaan wala teer , kade khind ke asman ch , uhdi umeed tor jave,


vekh tute teer mirja , dil ch rowe kurlawe , sahiba ne kita pith te waar , hun nazar na milawe , hoeya bebas , likhi apni de age ena , ik bol kaura , sahiba nu keha vina jawe ,


khalo gea mitti nu , mathe utte lake ,panja paniya da yodha , ikala panja nal hun zor azmawe, sahe kine e waar jatt siga khudaar , nai mareya bhrava to , sahiba de dokhe to kileya jave


maut de kande peya mirja , dil apne nu samjawe , kade himat ni pae kise di , jo hath mainu pawe , tere piche lag ke , jaan main apni gawae , rub kolo manga ehi , sahiba tere te dukh na koi awe...........

mithi boor nal khushboo , jado mitti d awe ,............................
