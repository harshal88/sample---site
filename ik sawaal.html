written by me (H.J)


ik sawal kado da mere zehan ch hai
puch chukeya murtaba hazaar
shayad farak mere kuch kehan ch hai.......


kyu ginti na mukdi tareya di
ki ehsas ,
samundar di lehar nal wehan ch hai


is zameen ne sanjoe kis lae eh kaeynat
koi hai jagah ,
jithe sukh parivar to wakh rehan ch hai


jazbatan de bawandar ne gheriya kiniya nu
kra deve jo salah ,
kabliyat kere alfaz kehan ch hai


kyu dagga kuma jande loak apaneyan de nal
kini chuupi majboori ,
kise diyan nazaran to lehan ch hai


ki sukoon ae dinda kore ch nigi dhup da saek
kina dukh gareebi ch ,
jaeth haar diyan dhupan sehan ch hai


kyu parindeya nu mili azadi asmaan di
kiniya mushkila ,
samaj diyan baediyan nal rehan ch hai


vehma bharma ne kinj kar dita khokhla buniyad nu
anjaan ne kini samae ,
kudrat ik raat de grehan ch hai


ki sochke ussarde hoan ge ameeran lae mehal
jina vichareya nu fikar ,
apna mitti da ghar dehan ch hai


ik sawal kado da mere zehan ch hai
puch chukeya murtaba hazaar
shayad farak mere kuch kehan ch hai .........
