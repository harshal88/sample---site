written by me (H.J)


tere pind nu jaandi sadak ni
kahto apne wal nu khichdi ae


ki rishta mera ehna sadraa nal
kyun uthe manzil disdi ae


painda kina ku lamba aje ishque da
dekho kithe yadaan wali chadar wichdi ae


rah ch lage rukh dhareek de haani mere
jina hatho haard di dhup ch chaan muft wikdi ae


jandi kachi nehar jo tere pind wal nu
bhare gawahi kande ude jeri koyla di mehfil sajdi ae


upparo suke rukhan di tahni deve awaaz mainu
us kone kudrat di wanjali jo nit wajdi ae


teri gali de ageo langdeya ni
kahto kadma di takat mukdi ae


kyun la laendiya palkan meriyan deera uthe
jithe naina di baari nu teri parchaee disdi ae


kiniyan rataan jagaeya teri yaad ne
ilam na hisab koi kithe tak kiti tareyan di ginti mukdi ae


kihne pal ne khushi de mere zehan ch samaye
jere futt ne sanjoye peer una di v dil ch kite risdi ae


kis gal da mooh mainu toori janda
dil wich ajehi awaaz vi uthadi ae


kine tup lae toye kine kande ne latare
wagdi looh di hawa kar tichara puchdi ae ...........


tere pind nu jaandi sadak ni
kahto apne wal nu khichdi ae


ki rishta mera ehna sadraa nal
kyun uthe manzil disdi ae ..................
